Laboratory Turnkey Services

LabCreator is a leading provider of turnkey laboratory solutions for various industries such as pharmaceutical, biotechnology, academic research, and clinical testing. Our turnkey services working process includes :

Consultation and Planning

Our team of experts will work with you to understand your specific needs and requirements, and develop a customized plan for your laboratory.

Design and Engineering

Our in-house design and engineering team will create a detailed layout and design for your laboratory, including equipment selection, installation, and integration.

Equipment Procurement

We have partnerships with leading manufacturers to provide you with high-quality equipment at competitive prices.

Installation and Commissioning

Our experienced installation team will install and commission your equipment, ensuring that everything is functioning properly.

Training and Support

We provide comprehensive training to your staff on the use of the equipment and software, as well as ongoing support to ensure that your laboratory is operating efficiently.

Project Management

Our project management team will oversee the entire process from start to finish, ensuring that the project is completed on time and within budget.

At LabCreator, we pride ourselves on our ability to provide turn-key solutions that are tailored to our clients' unique needs.
